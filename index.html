About Me

I design and build intelligent systems.

I'm Harshwardhan Pandey, an AI systems engineer and builder with a deep fascination for the structures — both digital and physical — that shape how we live and think. My work sits at the intersection of machine intelligence, thoughtful design, and rigorous engineering.

Over the past several years, I've designed and deployed production-grade machine learning pipelines, retrieval systems, and autonomous agents. I care about systems that are not only capable, but legible — software whose behavior can be reasoned about, trusted, and refined over time.

I approach building the way an architect approaches a site: with attention to constraints, context, and the quiet decisions that determine whether something endures. Whether it's a distributed inference layer or a small internal tool, I believe the same principles apply — clarity, intention, and craft.
