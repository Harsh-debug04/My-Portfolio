VISION & PHILOSOPHY

Built end-to-end for smallholder farmers who previously had no access to expert agricultural advisory. I designed and implemented a Spring Boot REST microservice backend, Vue.js 3 frontend with AG Grid advisory tables, and a multi-agent LangChain RAG pipeline over 12,000 ICAR instruction-output pairs.

Replacing a ₹2,000/visit agronomist consultation with a sub-2s query response at 50 concurrent users. Chose chunked streaming over full-response delivery after profiling revealed 2.1s median latency on batch mode — cutting perceived response time to under 400ms at p50; added circuit-breaker retry logic and Docker/Kubernetes orchestration, all designed and implemented independently.
