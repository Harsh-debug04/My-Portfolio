The Philosophy

The acquisition of the South Coast Estate represents more than a financial transaction; it is the cornerstone of our family's long-term environmental stewardship and health sanctuary. This milestone focuses on the intersection of regenerative architecture and holistic wellness.

By restoring the heritage orchards and implementing a private micro-grid, the estate will serve as a self-sustaining ecosystem. We are designing a space that dictates a slower tempo of life, where the architecture itself encourages reflection, movement, and connection to the coastal elements.
